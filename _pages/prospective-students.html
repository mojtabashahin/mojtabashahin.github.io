The projects mainly target Software Engineering for AI (SE4AI) and AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE).

Research Theme 1. Engineering AI-based Systems

This research theme aims to support engineering AI-based systems by conducting empirical studies and developing practical solutions, design principles, and tools. More specifically, the research theme aims to develop a deep understanding of the development and deployment of AI-based systems and develops practical solutions to design, evaluate, and monitor AI-based systems.

Research Theme 2. Human Values and Human-centric Issues in AI-based Systems

The goal of this research theme is to develop and evaluate well-established AI/LLM techniques to effectively detect, track, and classify human values and human-centric issues in AI-based systems by mining a wider range of software artifacts, such as source codes and developer discussions captured in code comments and issue tracking systems.

Research Theme 3. Socio-technical Aspects of Securing AI-based Systems

This research theme aims to investigate security in AI-based systems from a socio-technical perspective. Specifically, it explores how and when developers should integrate and practice security in AI-based systems, how developers use security tools and security-related code recommended by AI tools, and what socio-technical factors impact developers while making security decisions.
